Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This application ("App") is an internal financial reporting tool operated by Level Forward, Inc. ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with the App.

Data Collection

The App connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API to retrieve financial data belonging solely to Level Forward, Inc. and its subsidiaries. No personal data belonging to third parties is collected.

Data Use

Financial data retrieved through the App is used exclusively for internal reporting, analysis, and business operations. Data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely in a private database accessible only to authorized personnel of Level Forward, Inc.

Third Party Services

The App uses the Intuit QuickBooks API. Use of QuickBooks Online is subject to Intuit's own privacy policy.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at hs@levelforward.co.
